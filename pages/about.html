About Mgrand Sacco

Empowering our members through innovative financial solutions since 2015

Our Story

From humble beginnings to a thriving financial community

Mgrand Sacco was established in 2015 with a vision to provide affordable financial services to our community. What started as a small group of 50 members has grown to over 5,000 members today.

Our journey has been marked by steady growth, innovation, and a commitment to our members' financial wellbeing. We've weathered economic challenges and celebrated numerous milestones together with our members.

Today, we stand as a testament to what can be achieved when people come together with a common purpose of financial empowerment and community development.

Our Mission

To mobilize savings and provide affordable credit facilities to our members, while promoting financial literacy and economic empowerment in our community.

Our Vision

To be the leading SACCO in Kenya, recognized for excellence in service delivery, innovation, and member satisfaction.
